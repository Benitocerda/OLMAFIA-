Training Tiers

From movement quality to elite refinement — choose the tier that matches your current phase and goals.

See Details Ask for Placement

Overview

Placement considers age, experience, movement quality, and strength standards. We progress athletes between tiers as consistency and metrics improve.

Foundation

For new or returning athletes rebuilding mechanics. Sessions emphasize hip mobility, first-step explosiveness, bracing, sprint mechanics, and sled work. Exit standards include consistent stance/starts, base width control, and recovery footwork.

  • 2x/week small-group
  • Movement screens & micro-dosage strength
  • Home pillars: ankle/hip mobility, core

Gray

Position-specific craft: hands, strike timing, recoveries, independent drills and combo work. Add volume for power and eccentric control. Athletes track sets/reps and RPE.

  • 3x/week training rhythm
  • Film IQ module weekly
  • Strength focus: front squat, hinge, press

Black

Invite-only elite refinement and leadership. Detailed workload tracking, velocity awareness (bar speed), and competitive reps. Athletes mentor lower tiers and lead warm-ups.

  • 3–4x/week + film
  • Position mastery & protection schemes
  • Leadership & standards
